Causes


A few main causes for lack of clean dinking water

  1. Overuse of water
  2. Wastage of water
  3. Misuse of water
  4. Water Pollution
  5. Illegal Dumping
  6. Global Warming

Effects

  1. Cholera
  2. Typhoid
  3. Diarhea diseases
  4. Reduced ability for soils to support crops
  5. Increased amount of dust
  6. Erosion
  7. Greater chance of wildfires
